### Release 1.1.0 [2026/]
**Features:**
- Added lots of new Items(**Resonator of Spell**, **Potion of Purification**, **Starlight Ingot**...).

**Changes:**
- Books in the **Antique Book Bag** will now display descriptions of their relevant buffs.
- Adjusted the effects of **The Architect's Favor**.
- Improved the AI behavior of **Purified Ichor Spirit**.

**Fixes:**
- Fixed a bug where **The Cube** might cause the server to forcibly shut down.
- Fixed the Night Vision effect of the **Charm of Treasure Hunter** not working properly.
- Fixed some minor issues.

### Release 1.0.0 [2026/05/10]
**Features:**
- Most of the 1.20 content from **Enigmatic Legacy** and **Enigmatic Addons** has been moved to 1.21.
- Added lots of new contents and adjusted some original contents to fit new ones.
- Added **Config GUI** in game for easier modification.
- Added a compat item for **Thirst**(**The Forbidden Juice**).

**Changes:**
- Adjusted the tooltip color of cursed items.
- Adjusted the supported items of **Slayer**.
- Adjusted some text which does not affect the actual effect.
- Added a new feature of **The Burden of Desolation**.
- Slightly shortened the usage time of the **Twisted Mirror**.
- Added a linkage effect between **Ring of Quenching** and **Charm of Hell Blade**.
- Some items can be viewed for more details, such as **The Curse of Violence**.
- Adjusted the enchantments of tools.

**Fixes:**
- Fixed the issues of the tooltip of **Cursed Scroll**.
- Fixed some errors on textures and data.
- Fixed the potential error when saving **Extradimensional Vessel**.
- Fixed the error where the **Bulwark of Blazing Pride** could not extinguish the fire correctly.
- Fixed the issue where some items did apply additional damage on players.
- Fixed some issues that could cause fake death.
